OUR
STORY

The Beginning

Zero Gravity was born from a simple belief: digital experiences should be extraordinary, not ordinary. In 2009, a group of engineers and designers came together with a shared vision to create a company that would challenge the status quo and set new standards in web development.

Our Evolution

What started as a small studio has grown into a global digital innovation powerhouse. We've evolved from building simple websites to engineering complex, scalable platforms that serve millions of users. Our growth has been fueled by an unwavering commitment to excellence and innovation.

Today & Tomorrow

Today, we stand at the forefront of digital innovation, combining cutting-edge technology with human-centered design. We're not just building for today—we're engineering the future. With offices across four continents and a diverse team of 50+ experts, we're ready to tackle the world's most challenging digital problems.

Our Impact

We've helped startups become unicorns, transformed legacy systems for Fortune 500 companies, and created digital experiences that have reached over 100 million users worldwide. But numbers only tell part of the story—our real impact is measured in the success of our clients and the lives touched by the products we create.

OUR
JOURNEY

2009

The Foundation

Zero Gravity was founded in Bengaluru by three passionate developers with a vision to revolutionize web development. Our first office was a small studio, but our ambitions were limitless.

2012

First Major Milestone

Reached 100 completed projects and expanded our team to 15 members. Launched our first enterprise-scale platform serving over 1 million users, establishing our reputation for handling complex challenges.

2015

Global Expansion

Opened our New York office and established international partnerships. Won our first industry award for Best Digital Innovation, validating our approach and methodology.

2018

Innovation Lab Launch

Established our R&D division focused on emerging technologies like AI, blockchain, and AR/VR. Launched our proprietary development framework that dramatically improved project delivery times.

2020

Digital Transformation

Helped over 50 businesses successfully navigate digital transformation during the pandemic. Expanded to London and Tokyo, becoming a truly global operation with 24/7 support capabilities.

2024

Present Day

Today, we're a 50+ person team across four continents, having completed 500+ projects for clients worldwide. We continue to push boundaries, embracing new technologies and methodologies to deliver exceptional results.
